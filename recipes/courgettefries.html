Courgette Fries

Description

Courgette Fries are strips of fried courgette that can be used in place of regular potoato fries for a healtheir option.

Ingredients

  • 2 Courgettes, cut intro strips
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • oil for frying
  • Cheese for topping

Steps

  1. Place flour in bowl and season with salt and pepper
  2. Mix with a little water to form batter, place in cut courgettes and coat in batter
  3. Heat oil to 200c and carefully lower fries into oil until completely covered
  4. Cook for around 5 minutes until browning, then remove with slotted spoon and drain on kitchen towel
  5. Grate cheese on top, serve
