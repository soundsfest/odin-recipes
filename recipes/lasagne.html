Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a baked italian dish that is super delicious and full of deliciousness

Ingredients

  • 15 Lasagne Sheets
  • 2 Spoons Olive Olive
  • 300g Chopped Mushroom
  • 500ml Tomato Sauce
  • 300g grated cheese
  • Salt
  • 150g washed spinach
  • 1 Teaspoon Oregano
  • 1 Teaspoon Basil
  • 300g Vege Mince
  • 1 Jar of cheese sauce
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta in salty water for 5 mins and drain
  2. Pre-heat oven to 200c
  3. Saute garlic and onion in oil for 5 mins
  4. Add Mince, mushrooms and fry on low heat for 15 minutes
  5. Add tomato sauce, basil, oregano
  6. Layer pasta sheets with spinach and mince mixture alternately in dish, top with cheese sauce and then cheese and put in oven
  7. Remove from oven after 40 mins
